# Privacy Policy for QR Pro - QR Code Scanner & Generator

**Last updated: January 1, 2026**

**WesLakInc** ("we", "our", or "us") operates the QR Pro mobile application (the "App").

## Information Collection and Use

### Camera Access
The App requires camera access solely to scan QR codes and barcodes. Camera data is processed locally on your device and is **never transmitted, stored, or shared** with any third parties.

### Internet Access
The App uses internet access for:
- **URL Safety Checking**: When you scan a URL QR code, the App may check the URL against safety databases to warn you about potentially malicious websites. This check is performed anonymously.
- **Advertisements**: The App displays ads provided by Google AdMob.

### Local Storage
The App stores your scan history and generated QR codes locally on your device. This data:
- Remains entirely on your device
- Is never uploaded to external servers
- Can be deleted at any time through the App settings

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Contact information
- Device identifiers for tracking purposes
- Any scanned QR code content (beyond local storage on your device)

## Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob may collect certain information as described in [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App or on this page.
