# Privacy Policy for Flappy Duck

**Last updated: January 4, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to Flappy Duck! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile game application ("App").

## Information We Collect

### Information Collected Automatically

When you use Flappy Duck, we may automatically collect:

- **Game Data**: High scores, coins earned, power-up levels, and game progress
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: Game sessions, features used, and in-game actions

### Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

#### Google Play Games Services
- Used for leaderboards and achievements
- Collects your Google Play Games profile information
- Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

#### Google AdMob
- Used to display advertisements
- May collect device identifiers and usage data for personalized ads
- Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Save your game progress locally on your device
- Display your scores on leaderboards
- Track achievement progress
- Show relevant advertisements
- Improve game performance and user experience

## Data Storage

- **Local Data**: Game progress (high scores, coins, settings) is stored locally on your device
- **Cloud Data**: Leaderboard scores and achievements are stored through Google Play Games Services

## Children's Privacy

Flappy Duck is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game does not require account creation or personal data input.

## Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads in the game. AdMob may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our app or other apps. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google's Ad Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads).

## Your Rights

You can:

- **Delete Local Data**: Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
- **Opt-out of Personalized Ads**: Adjust your device's ad preferences
- **Disconnect Google Play Games**: Manage your connection through Google Play Games settings

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
